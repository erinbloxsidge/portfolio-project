About me

Background

I have a MSc in Library Science from City University London and I've spent the past 6 years working in Higher Education in the UK. I've taught students of all levels information literacy skills and I am a Fellow of Advance HE (FHEA). My current role focuses on helping academic departments embed library services into their teaching through relationship management and learning technologies.

Interests

As someone who considers themself a lifelong learner, I have several hobbies I love doing deep dives into. I'll talk your ear off about my favourite oolong teas, what yarns I love to knit with, and what my latest addition to my houseplant collection is. I've been known to nerd out over weird local folklore and frequently spend my weekends with my head burried in a good book.
